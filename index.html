Research Interest: Cybersecurity, IoT Security, Vulnerability Research, Binary Code Fingerprinting and Malware Analysis, Threat Intelligence Generation, Machine Learning.
I am an Assistant Professor at the School of School of Electrical Engineering and Computer Science (EECS) at University of Ottawa. Prior to this, I was an Assistant Professor at the Department of Computer Science at Toronto Metropolitan University (formerly known as Ryerson University). I hold a PhD degree in Information System Engineering from Concordia University.

I am committed to providing a training and reserach environment built on the fundamental values of Equity, Diversity and Inclusion (EDI). I have multiple funded positions at the Undergrad, Masters, Ph.D., and Postdoctoral levels. I am looking for motivated candidates with strong academic background and technical skills in cybersecurity and/or machine learning. To apply, please email me your CV and your transcripts. You will only hear from me, if I am interested in your application. Please consult Computer Science Graduate Program, and Admission Regulation Admission Regulation for more information.
